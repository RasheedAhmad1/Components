LG Field Formations

Directorate General
Local Government Department is mandated with administration and management of the local government institutions in the province. Different entities including the Directorate General along with 35 offices of Assistant Directors, LG&RDD are working under the Administrative control of Secretary, LG&RDD.
Local Council Board
The core function of the Local Council Board is to cater for services including appointments, promotions, postings, transfers, pensions, etc. of the personnel paid out of the Local Fund. It is the successor of the defunct NWFP Local Government Board created under the Basic Democracies Orders 1959 and Municipal Administration Ordinance 1960.
Urban Area Development Authorities
Specialized autonomous bodies for housing needs of the urban population 10 authorities in different cities providing the following functions:
  • Housing townships in different cities
  • Master planning for housing societies under their control
  • Provision of infrastructure and civic facilities such as water supply, drainage, waste management
  • Construction and maintenance of roads
  • Provision of streetlights
  • Parking and development of parks in the entire townships besides, planning & zoning of un-planned areas into new resorts/township schemes
Water And Sanitation Services Companies
Specialized autonomous bodies for housing needs of the urban population 10 authorities in different cities providing the following functions:
  • Special purpose vehicles for critical public services in urban centers
  • Established under section 115A of LGA 2013
  • Established as companies under Companies Act 2017 in 7 divisional headquarters
  • Working under a corporate governance model
  • Providing the following specialized services:
    • Provision of clean drinking water
    • Solid waste management
    • Spatial planning
    • Sewerage and drainage management
